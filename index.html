About the Event

Tech Innovations in Nepal: Connecting Locally, Expanding Globally is a one-day event dedicated to exploring the potential of technology in Nepal’s future. Organized by N4IT • New Frontier in Tech, this conference brings together industry leaders, policymakers, innovators, and tech enthusiasts to discuss the role of technology in driving economic growth, improving education, and transforming Nepal into a regional innovation hub.

The goal of this event is to create a collaborative environment where diverse perspectives come together to inspire action and foster new connections. Key topics will include government support for tech, investment opportunities, skill-building, and digital inclusion. Join us for a day of insightful discussions, workshops, and networking, where we envision Nepal’s digital future.

About the Summit


"Tech Innovations in Nepal" is a unique opportunity for participants to gain insights from VIP speakers, including Nepal’s political leaders, and engage with thought leaders from the tech industry. With sessions focused on entrepreneurship, digital transformation, and technology’s role in economic development, this summit will empower participants to become active contributors to Nepal’s tech landscape.
